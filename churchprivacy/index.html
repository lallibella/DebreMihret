Privacy Policy for Debre Mihret St Michael Church

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Debre Mihret St Michael Church is a local-first app for managing church service schedules, servants, assignments, tasks, and optional Telegram reminders.

Information Stored in the App

The app may store information that you enter, including church name, servant names, phone numbers, roles, notes, task assignments, schedules, reminder details, Telegram bot settings, Telegram chat IDs, language preferences, and app lock settings.

This information is stored locally on your device. The app does not use a developer-operated cloud database or account system.

Local Security

Debre Mihret St Michael Church encrypts its local app data on the device. The app may also use Face ID, Touch ID, device passcode, or an app PIN to help protect access. Biometric authentication is handled by iOS; Debre Mihret St Michael Church does not receive or store your biometric data.

Backups

The app allows you to export an encrypted backup file. You choose the backup password and control where the backup file is stored or shared. Backup files may contain church data, servant details, schedules, and Telegram settings, so they should only be shared with authorized organizers.

Telegram

Telegram integration is optional. If you configure Telegram reminders, the app communicates directly with Telegram's API using the bot token you provide. This may send Telegram chat IDs, reminder text, manual message text, and related scheduling information to Telegram so messages can be delivered.

Telegram's handling of that data is governed by Telegram's own privacy practices. Debre Mihret St Michael Church does not operate a separate server to store Telegram data.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not use advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or third-party analytics SDKs. We do not sell personal data. We do not collect location, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or health data.

Payments

If Debre Mihret St Michael Church is sold as a paid app, purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your payment card details.

Data Control

Because the app is local-first, most data is controlled directly on your device. You can delete app data by deleting the app from your device. You can also stop using Telegram features by removing Telegram bot settings or revoking your bot token through Telegram/BotFather.

Children

Debre Mihret St Michael Church is intended for church organizers and administrators. It is not directed to children.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this page.
